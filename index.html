AI Product Studio Launch →

Generate professional product photography through AI conversation.

Key Features:

  • Multi-image upload support (1-4 images, first becomes primary reference)
  • Intelligent product recreation with automatic background mode fallback
  • Logo and artwork placement capabilities
  • Optimized for both individual items and garments

How to use:

Upload between 1-4 images of your product here to analyze and start. For items, 1 image of the product in a hand or body part is a great way to give the AI size reference. For garments, a flat lay works great. If the system misunderstands the product or any elements like a collar or sleeve type, just tell the AI the missing element to fix in image generation. Great also for placing logos and artworks onto products like T-shirts etc.

Great for:

Social media and ecommerce photography. Excels at garment photography and model shots from flat lays, table shots of your product, logo placement on products.
